Complaint Letter

What is it? A letter to address problems or dissatisfaction with products, services, or situations.

Common Lines: I am writing to express my concern... / I would like to bring to your notice...

Common Topics: Faulty goods, poor service, public nuisance.

PYQs: Write a complaint to the Municipal Corporation about the unclean environment in your area.

Tips: Be polite, mention details, include a request for resolution.

Example:

To,
The Municipal Commissioner,
ABC Nagar, Mumbai.

Subject: Complaint regarding unclean surroundings in ABC Nagar

Sir/Madam,

I am writing this letter to bring to your attention the extremely unhygienic conditions prevailing in our locality of ABC Nagar. The drains are overflowing, garbage remains uncollected for days, and the streetlights are not working at several places.

This situation is creating severe health hazards. Many residents are falling sick, especially children and the elderly. The foul smell and stagnant water have made it unbearable for us to even step out.

I request you to take immediate action in cleaning our area, sending garbage trucks regularly, and repairing the drainage and lighting issues. Hoping for a prompt resolution to make our surroundings clean and healthy again.

Yours sincerely,
Sanskar Shep

Leave Application

What is it? A formal request to get permission for leave from school or office.

Common Lines: I am writing to request leave... / Kindly grant me...

Common Topics: Sick leave, family function, exam preparation.

PYQs: Write an application to your Principal requesting three days of leave due to illness.

Tips: Be concise, mention reason and duration, use polite tone.

Example:

To,
The Principal,
XYZ School, Pune.

Subject: Application for sick leave

Sir/Madam,

I am Sanskar Shep, a student of Class 10, writing to request three days' leave from school as I am suffering from viral fever. My doctor has advised complete rest to recover properly.

I will ensure to catch up on all missed assignments and lessons. Kindly grant me leave from 1st to 3rd August 2025.

I shall be grateful for your kind consideration.

Thanking you,
Yours obediently,
Sanskar Shep

Request Letter

What is it? A letter used to make a formal request to a higher authority.

Common Lines: I would like to request... / Kindly allow me...

Common Topics: Issue of books, certificates, internet facility, permission.

PYQs: Request your librarian to issue a new library card.

Tips: Write clearly, use formal tone, explain purpose.

Example:

To,
The Librarian,
XYZ School, Beed.

Subject: Request for issuing a new library card

Sir/Madam,

I am Sanskar Shep of Class 10. I have unfortunately lost my library card and am unable to borrow any new books. I request you to kindly issue a duplicate card.

I understand the importance of following rules and will be more careful in the future. I am ready to pay the required fee for the new card if needed.

Kindly consider my request and issue the new card at your earliest convenience.

Thanking you,
Yours sincerely,
Sanskar Shep

Order Letter

What is it? A letter written to place an official order for goods or services.

Common Lines: Please supply the following... / We would like to order...

Common Topics: School materials, books, furniture, sports goods.

PYQs: Write a letter to M/s Ajay Stationers to order stationery items for your school.

Tips: Clearly mention item names, quantity, address, and delivery details.

Example:

To,
M/s Ajay Stationers,
Pune, Maharashtra.

Subject: Order for supply of stationery items

Dear Sir/Madam,

We wish to place an order for the following items for our school: 20 dozen notebooks, 10 packs of pens, 5 boxes of chalk, and 15 registers. Kindly ensure that all items are of good quality.

Please deliver the goods to XYZ School, Ambajogai, within 7 days of receiving this letter. The payment will be made after proper inspection.

We hope for prompt and quality service from your side. Kindly confirm the order as soon as possible.

Yours faithfully,
Sanskar Shep

Letter to the Editor

What is it? A formal letter written to a newspaper editor about public issues or opinions.

Common Lines: Through the columns of your esteemed newspaper... / I wish to highlight...

Common Topics: Pollution, road safety, electricity issues, public health.

PYQs: Write a letter to the editor about increasing noise pollution in your city.

Tips: Be specific, give examples, end with a suggestion or request.

Example:

To,
The Editor,
The Times of India,
Mumbai.

Subject: Rising noise pollution in the city

Sir/Madam,

Through the columns of your esteemed newspaper, I want to draw attention to the problem of rising noise pollution in our city. The situation is worsening due to excessive use of loudspeakers and traffic horns.

People, especially students and elderly, are suffering from stress and disturbed sleep. Despite rules, no action seems to be taken by the authorities.

I request concerned departments to take strict steps and spread awareness to control this issue. I hope my voice will reach the responsible officials.

Yours sincerely,
Sanskar Shep

Enquiry Letter

What is it? A letter written to ask for details about courses, events, products, or services.

Common Lines: I am interested in... / Kindly provide the following information...

Common Topics: Course enquiry, price enquiry, accommodation details.

PYQs: Write a letter enquiring about summer coaching classes in science and maths.

Tips: Be polite, ask specific questions, and request brochures or details.

Example:

To,
The Director,
Bright Future Academy,
Pune.

Subject: Enquiry about science and maths summer coaching

Sir/Madam,

I came across your advertisement about summer coaching classes in science and maths for Class 10 students. I am very interested in enrolling and want to know the following details.

Please share the course duration, fees, class timings, faculty qualifications, and mode of teaching (online/offline).

Kindly send me the brochure or reply via email. I look forward to your response so I can make an informed decision.

Thanking you,
Yours sincerely,
Sanskar Shep
