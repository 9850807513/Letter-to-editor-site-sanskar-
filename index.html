Letter to the Editor

What is it? A formal letter sent to a newspaper editor expressing opinion or concern about public issues.

Common Lines: I would like to draw the attention of the concerned authorities / I hope my views will find some space in your prestigious newspaper.

Common Topics: Pollution, road safety, electricity issues, awareness about health, education system, etc.

PYQ: Write a letter to the editor of a national daily expressing concern about the increasing junk food consumption among teenagers. (CBSE 2020)

Tips: Be precise, express your opinion respectfully, suggest solutions.

Example:

Subject: Rising pollution due to vehicle emissions

The Editor,
The Times of India,
Mumbai.

Respected Sir/Madam,

Through the columns of your esteemed newspaper, I would like to bring to the attention of the public and authorities the serious problem of air pollution caused by increasing vehicular traffic. In cities like ours, every day more vehicles are added to the roads, leading to heavy traffic jams and poor air quality. The harmful gases released from these vehicles are affecting people's health, especially children and the elderly.

I urge the authorities to encourage public transport and promote electric vehicles. Strict regulations and awareness campaigns can help reduce this issue. I hope this concern will receive the attention it deserves.

Thanking you.
Yours sincerely,
Sanskar Shep

Complaint Letter

What is it? A letter written to express dissatisfaction regarding a service or product.

Common Lines: I am writing to bring to your notice a serious issue / Kindly take immediate action in this regard.

Common Topics: Defective product, poor service, misconduct by staff, etc.

PYQ: Write a letter to the Manager of a store complaining about the defective washing machine you purchased. (CBSE 2018)

Tips: Be polite, clearly describe the issue, request prompt action.

Example:

Subject: Complaint regarding defective washing machine

The Manager,
Electronic World,
Pune.

Respected Sir/Madam,

I purchased a Whirlpool washing machine from your store on 10th June 2025. However, within a week, the machine stopped functioning properly. It makes loud noises and fails to spin clothes effectively. Despite repeated attempts to reach your customer service, no assistance has been provided so far.

I request you to send a technician for inspection or replace the product as per the warranty terms. I hope this matter is addressed urgently to avoid further inconvenience.

Thanking you.
Yours sincerely,
Sanskar Shep

Letter of Inquiry

What is it? A letter written to ask for information about a course, service, event, or product.

Common Lines: I wish to inquire about / Kindly provide details regarding...

Common Topics: Courses, hotel bookings, training programs, etc.

PYQ: Write a letter to the Director of British Council, Delhi, seeking information about spoken English classes. (CBSE 2016)

Tips: Ask specific questions, be formal, request a reply.

Example:

Subject: Inquiry about spoken English course

The Director,
British Council,
Delhi.

Respected Sir/Madam,

I am a Class 10 student and wish to improve my spoken English skills. I have heard about your reputed courses and would like to enroll in one. Kindly provide me with details regarding course duration, fees, batch timings, and the registration procedure.

It would be great if you could also inform me about any weekend or online options available. I look forward to your prompt response.

Thanking you.
Yours sincerely,
Sanskar Shep

Request Letter

What is it? A letter written to formally request something from an authority.

Common Lines: I would like to request you to / I shall be grateful if...

Common Topics: Permission for leave, change in section, fee concession, etc.

PYQ: Write a letter to your principal requesting permission to organize a science exhibition. (CBSE 2019)

Tips: Be respectful, state reason clearly, and thank in advance.

Example:

Subject: Request for organizing a science exhibition

The Principal,
ABC School,
Beed.

Respected Sir/Madam,

I am the Head of the Science Club of Class 10. I would like to request permission to organize a science exhibition on the occasion of National Science Day. The aim is to promote scientific temperament among students through working models and charts.

We will follow all necessary guidelines and ensure discipline. We request your approval and support for the same.

Thanking you.
Yours sincerely,
Sanskar Shep

Order Letter

What is it? A letter written to place an order for goods or services.

Common Lines: Kindly deliver the following items at the earliest / We expect timely delivery...

Common Topics: Books, lab equipment, sports items, uniforms, etc.

PYQ: Write a letter to a bookseller placing an order for textbooks for Class 10. (CBSE 2015)

Tips: Mention quantity, product names, delivery address, payment mode.

Example:

Subject: Order for textbooks of Class 10

The Sales Manager,
National Book House,
Mumbai.

Respected Sir/Madam,

We wish to place an order for the following textbooks for our school library. Please find the list below:

  • Science – 10 copies
  • Mathematics – 10 copies
  • English – 10 copies
  • Social Science – 10 copies

Please ensure the books are delivered within 7 working days. Payment will be made upon delivery.

Thanking you.
Yours sincerely,
Sanskar Shep

Job Application

What is it? A letter written to apply for a job along with a resume/bio-data.

Common Lines: With reference to your advertisement / I wish to offer my candidature...

Common Topics: Teacher, librarian, receptionist, assistant, etc.

PYQ: Write an application for the post of receptionist in a reputed company. (CBSE 2020)

Tips: Attach bio-data, use formal tone, highlight relevant skills.

Example:

Subject: Application for the post of receptionist

The HR Manager,
XYZ Pvt Ltd,
Pune.

Respected Sir/Madam,

With reference to your advertisement in The Times of India dated 15th July 2025, I wish to apply for the post of receptionist in your esteemed company. I have completed my higher secondary education and possess good communication skills, fluency in English, and basic computer knowledge.

I enclose my bio-data for your kind consideration. I shall be grateful for an opportunity to work in your organization.

Thanking you.
Yours sincerely,
Sanskar Shep
