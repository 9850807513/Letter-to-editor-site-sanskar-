Letter to the Editor

Definition: A letter written to a newspaper or magazine editor to express opinions or raise public concerns.

Common Lines: "Through your esteemed newspaper, I want to draw attention to..."

Common Topics: Pollution, Road Safety, Student Pressure

Tips: Be concise, polite, and focus on solutions.

Example:

To,
The Editor,
The Times of India,
Mumbai.

Subject: Growing Menace of Plastic Pollution

Respected Sir/Madam,

I am Sanskar Shep, a concerned student and environmental enthusiast, writing to you to express my concern about the increasing plastic pollution in our city. Every street corner is littered with plastic bags and bottles, which not only spoil the environment but also endanger the lives of animals and marine life.

This situation has worsened over the last few years due to lack of strict regulations and awareness. Most people continue using plastic despite the ban due to the absence of alternatives and weak enforcement. Local authorities must impose heavy penalties on violators and initiate cleanup drives regularly.

I request you to highlight this issue in your esteemed newspaper so that awareness spreads among the masses and actions are taken by the authorities. A plastic-free environment is not just a dreamâ€”it is a necessity. Your support can make a significant difference.

Sincerely,
Sanskar Shep

Complaint Letter

Definition: A formal letter used to complain about poor service, defective products, or inconvenience.

Common Lines: "I am writing to bring to your notice..."

Common Topics: Faulty product, Irregular supply, Misbehavior

Tips: Keep it polite but firm, give facts.

Example:

To,
The Manager,
ABC Electronics,
Pune.

Subject: Complaint about Faulty Refrigerator

Respected Sir/Madam,

I am Sanskar Shep, a regular customer of your electronic store. I am writing this letter to complain about the refrigerator I purchased from your store on 20th July 2025. It has stopped cooling after just one week of use, and the service helpline is not responding.

This defect has caused great inconvenience in my household. As a customer, I expected better quality and service from your reputed brand. Despite repeated calls, no technician has visited for repair or replacement so far. This kind of negligence is unacceptable and disappointing.

I request you to either replace the product or refund the amount immediately. Please take urgent action to maintain your company's goodwill. I hope for a quick and positive response, failing which I may consider reporting this matter to consumer forums.

Yours sincerely,
Sanskar Shep

Enquiry Letter

Definition: A letter written to request information or clarification about a product, service, or course.

Common Lines: "I wish to enquire about..."

Common Topics: Courses, Products, Packages

Tips: Be specific in your query and polite in tone.

Example:

To,
The Director,
Smart Learning Institute,
Delhi.

Subject: Enquiry about English Speaking Course

Respected Sir/Madam,

I am Sanskar Shep, a student of Class 10, interested in joining your English Speaking course during my summer break. I came to know about your institute through an online advertisement and would like to gather some details before making a decision.

Please provide information regarding the course duration, batch timings, fees, and certification. I would also like to know about the availability of online classes, as I may not be able to attend offline sessions every day due to my school schedule.

I request you to kindly send me a brochure or the necessary details at the earliest. Your prompt response will help me plan accordingly and enhance my communication skills effectively.

Yours faithfully,
Sanskar Shep

Order Letter

Definition: A letter placed to order goods or services.

Common Lines: "I would like to place an order for..."

Common Topics: Books, Uniforms, Furniture

Tips: Mention quantity, price, and delivery details.

Example:

To,
The Sales Manager,
Rajat Book Depot,
Nagpur.

Subject: Order for Class 10 Textbooks

Respected Sir/Madam,

I am Sanskar Shep, in charge of the student council of Bluebell High School. I would like to place an order for textbooks required for the new academic session of Class 10. We have selected your store due to your timely deliveries and reasonable pricing.

Kindly send us 50 copies each of NCERT Science, Mathematics, English, and Social Science textbooks. Please ensure that the books are latest edition and well-packaged. The delivery should be made to the school premises within 7 working days of receiving this letter.

We hope for a smooth transaction and timely delivery. Please send the bill along with the consignment and confirm this order by return mail.

Sincerely,
Sanskar Shep

Leave Application

Definition: A formal request made to a higher authority for leave of absence.

Common Lines: "I request you to kindly grant me leave..."

Common Topics: Medical leave, Family function, Exam preparation

Tips: Mention dates and reason clearly, be polite.

Example:

To,
The Principal,
Sunflower Public School,
Aurangabad.

Subject: Application for Sick Leave

Respected Sir/Madam,

I am Sanskar Shep, a student of Class 10 B, writing this letter to request leave for three days from 29th July to 31st July 2025, as I am suffering from viral fever and the doctor has advised me complete rest during this period.

My parents have attached a medical certificate along with this application. I assure you that I will cover the syllabus missed during the leave and take help from my classmates to stay updated with classwork.

Kindly consider my request and grant me leave for the above-mentioned period. I shall be thankful for your understanding and cooperation.

Yours obediently,
Sanskar Shep

Request Application

Definition: A formal letter requesting permission, documents, or changes.

Common Lines: "I humbly request you to..."

Common Topics: Bonafide certificate, Change of section, Fee concession

Tips: Be respectful and explain the need clearly.

Example:

To,
The Principal,
Bright Future School,
Beed.

Subject: Request for Bonafide Certificate

Respected Sir/Madam,

I am Sanskar Shep, a student of Class 10 A. I require a bonafide certificate for submission to the passport office. The application process requires school verification, and I need this certificate urgently to complete my documentation.

I request you to kindly issue the bonafide certificate in my name, mentioning my enrollment details and current academic year. I am ready to submit any further documents if required by the school administration for this purpose.

I hope my request will be considered sympathetically and the certificate will be issued within this week. Thank you for your support.

Yours sincerely,
Sanskar Shep
