Letter to the Editor

What is it?
A formal letter written to the editor of a newspaper/magazine to raise awareness, express opinion, or suggest improvements.


Common Lines:
- Through the columns of your esteemed newspaper, I would like to draw attention to...
- I hope my letter will be published to bring attention to this concern.
- Kindly allow me space in your newspaper.


Common Topics:
Cleanliness, Road Safety, Water Wastage, Pollution, Public Transport, Corruption, Education System, Mobile Addiction.


CBSE PYQ:
"Write a letter to the editor highlighting the issue of rising pollution levels in your city." – CBSE 2020.


Important Tips:
- Stay formal and respectful
- Mention the issue clearly
- Suggest practical solutions
- Keep it short, clear, and relevant
