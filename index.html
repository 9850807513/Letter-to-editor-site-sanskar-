Letter to the Editor

What is it? A letter written to a newspaper/magazine editor to highlight a social issue or provide feedback/opinion.

Common Lines: Through the columns of your esteemed newspaper, I wish to draw attention to...

Common Topics: Pollution, road safety, public cleanliness, student pressure, traffic, electricity, etc.

CBSE PYQ: Write a letter to the editor about increasing noise pollution in your locality.

Tips: Be formal and polite, avoid personal attacks, suggest solutions.

Example:

Subject: Rising Noise Pollution in Our Locality

Dear Sir/Madam,

Through the columns of your esteemed newspaper, I wish to draw the attention of the concerned authorities towards the increasing problem of noise pollution in our area. The frequent honking of vehicles, loudspeakers, and construction work throughout the day and night has made it difficult for students, senior citizens, and patients to live peacefully. Despite repeated complaints, no strict actions have been taken so far. I request the concerned departments to take immediate steps to control this menace. Awareness campaigns, restrictions on unnecessary honking, and enforcement of noise rules can help greatly.

Yours sincerely,
Sanskar Shep

Job Application Letter

What is it? A formal letter sent with a resume for applying to a job.

Common Lines: With reference to your advertisement in The Times of India dated...

Common Topics: Teacher job, librarian, receptionist, technician.

CBSE PYQ: Write a job application for the post of a receptionist in a school.

Tips: Include qualifications, experience, and soft skills.

Example:

Subject: Application for the Post of Receptionist

Dear Sir/Madam,

With reference to your advertisement in The Times of India dated 12th July 2025, I wish to apply for the post of receptionist at your esteemed institution. I am a graduate with good communication and organizational skills. I have one year of experience handling front desk duties in a private firm. I am proficient in English and Hindi and have a pleasing personality. My resume is attached for your kind consideration.

Yours sincerely,
Sanskar Shep

Letter of Enquiry

What is it? A letter asking for information about a course, tour, product, etc.

Common Lines: I wish to make an enquiry regarding...

Common Topics: Enquiry about summer camp, coaching classes, hostel facilities, educational tours.

CBSE PYQ: Write a letter enquiring about a science summer camp.

Tips: Ask for details (duration, cost, facilities), be formal.

Example:

Subject: Enquiry About Summer Science Camp

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am a student of Class 10 and am interested in participating in your summer science camp. I would like to enquire about the duration of the camp, the topics covered, fee structure, accommodation, and eligibility. Please send me a brochure or detailed information at the earliest so that I may plan accordingly.

Yours faithfully,
Sanskar Shep

Letter of Order

What is it? A letter placing an order for goods/services.

Common Lines: We wish to place an order for the following items...

Common Topics: Books for school, sports equipment, lab materials.

CBSE PYQ: Write a letter placing an order for sports equipment.

Tips: Be clear, list items, mention quantity, payment mode.

Example:

Subject: Order for Sports Equipment

Dear Sir/Madam,

We are placing an order for the following sports items for our school: 10 footballs, 5 cricket kits, 6 badminton rackets, and 4 volleyballs. Kindly ensure the items are of good quality and delivered within a week. Payment will be made by cheque upon delivery.

Yours sincerely,
Sanskar Shep

Letter for Leave

What is it? A request letter asking for leave from school or work.

Common Lines: I request you to kindly grant me leave for...

Common Topics: Sick leave, family function, urgent personal work.

Tips: Mention exact dates, reason briefly, stay formal.

Example:

Subject: Request for Sick Leave

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am a student of Class 10. I have been suffering from viral fever and am advised complete bed rest for three days. Kindly grant me leave from 20th to 22nd July 2025. I assure you that I will cover all missed work.

Yours obediently,
Sanskar Shep

Letter of Request

What is it? A letter requesting permission, documents, certificates, or help.

Common Lines: I request you to kindly issue/provide/allow...

Common Topics: Request for certificate, duplicate mark sheet, permission to attend competition.

Tips: Be polite, specific, brief.

Example:

Subject: Request for Bonafide Certificate

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am a student of Class 10 of your school. I need a bonafide certificate for my scholarship application. I request you to kindly issue the same at the earliest.

Yours obediently,
Sanskar Shep
