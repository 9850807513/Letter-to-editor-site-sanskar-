Letter to the Editor

What is it?
A formal letter written to the editor of a newspaper or magazine to express opinion or raise concern about a public issue.

Common Lines:
I am writing to express my concern on... / Through the esteemed column of your newspaper...

Common Topics:
Pollution, Traffic issues, Lack of streetlights, Social awareness, Student stress.

CBSE PYQ Example:
Write a letter to the editor about increasing noise pollution in your city.

Tips: Be formal, stay to the point, avoid slang, suggest solutions.

Example:

24/2 Shanti Nagar
Beed, Maharashtra
24 July 2025

The Editor
The Times of India
Mumbai

Subject: Need to control noise pollution in urban areas

Sir,

I am writing to draw your attention to the growing problem of noise pollution in our city. The constant honking, loudspeakers during festivals, and construction activities are creating an unbearable environment. Children and elderly people are unable to rest or study properly. Despite rules being present, implementation is very weak. I urge the authorities through your respected newspaper to take strict action and create awareness among citizens to maintain peace and silence. A peaceful city is everyone’s right and responsibility.

Thank you

Yours sincerely,
Sanskar Shep

Complaint Letter

What is it?
A formal letter to lodge a complaint about poor service, defective products, etc.

Common Lines:
I wish to file a complaint regarding... / I am dissatisfied with the service...

Common Topics:
Defective product, poor service, damaged goods, delayed delivery.

CBSE PYQ Example:
Write a complaint to the manager of a showroom regarding a faulty mobile phone.

Tips: Include bill number, date of purchase, be polite but firm.

Example:

12 Gandhi Road
Beed, Maharashtra
10 February 2025

The Manager
Reliance Digital Showroom
Pune

Subject: Complaint about faulty mobile phone

Sir/Madam,

I purchased a Redmi Note 12 from your showroom on 1st February 2025. The phone has started malfunctioning within a week – the screen hangs and the battery drains quickly. I request you to kindly look into the issue and either replace the handset or initiate a refund as per your warranty policy. I am attaching the bill for your reference. I hope for a quick and fair resolution.

Thank you

Yours faithfully,
Sanskar Shep

Inquiry Letter

What is it?
A formal letter to ask about details of a course, trip, or program.

Common Lines:
I am interested in knowing more about... / Kindly provide the details regarding...

Common Topics:
Summer course, educational trip, school admission, hostel facility.

CBSE PYQ Example:
Write an inquiry to ABC Institute about a summer computer course.

Tips: Ask about fees, duration, timings, eligibility, contact details.

Example:

31 Main Bazaar
Beed, Maharashtra
15 March 2025

The Director
ABC Institute
Mumbai

Subject: Inquiry about summer computer course

Sir/Madam,

I am a student of Class 10 and I wish to join your summer computer course. I request you to kindly provide information about the course duration, fees, class timings, eligibility criteria, and certificate validity. I am very keen to enhance my computer knowledge and skills during the holidays. Please send the details to the above address or contact me at the given number.

Thank you

Yours sincerely,
Sanskar Shep

Placing Order Letter

What is it?
Written to order goods or services from a company.

Common Lines:
We wish to place an order for... / Kindly dispatch the following items...

Common Topics:
Books, sports items, school uniforms, furniture.

CBSE PYQ Example:
Write a letter placing an order for sports items for your school.

Tips: Mention quantity, product names, delivery date, payment terms.

Example:

Vidya Mandir School
Beed, Maharashtra
5 April 2025

The Manager
Sporty Goods Pvt. Ltd.
Nagpur

Subject: Order for sports items for school

Sir/Madam,

We wish to place an order for the following sports items for our annual sports day: 10 footballs, 5 volleyballs, 8 badminton rackets, 3 cricket kits, and 50 whistles. Kindly dispatch them to our school address by 15th April. Please ensure proper packaging and include the bill with GST for school accounts. Payment will be made after the delivery.

Thank you

Yours faithfully,
Sanskar Shep

Job Application Letter

What is it?
A formal letter applying for a job vacancy.

Common Lines:
I came across your advertisement... / I wish to apply for the position of...

Common Topics:
Teacher, typist, clerk, receptionist.

CBSE PYQ Example:
Write an application for the post of receptionist at a hotel.

Tips: Attach CV, highlight strengths, use formal tone, include contact info.

Example:

45 Nehru Nagar
Beed, Maharashtra
20 May 2025

The HR Manager
Taj Hotel
Mumbai

Subject: Application for the post of receptionist

Sir/Madam,

I came across your advertisement for the post of receptionist and would like to apply. I have excellent communication skills and basic knowledge of computer operations. I have completed Class 12 with distinction and am currently pursuing a diploma in Hospitality. I am sincere, punctual, and willing to take responsibility. I am attaching my resume with this application. I hope to get an opportunity to work in your prestigious organization.

Thank you

Yours sincerely,
Sanskar Shep
