All About Formal Letters

What Are Letters?

Letters are written forms of communication between individuals or organizations. They serve different purposes like expressing opinions, requesting information, lodging complaints, or placing orders.

Types of Formal Letters

  • Letter to the Editor
  • Complaint Letter
  • Enquiry Letter
  • Order Letter

Why Informal Letters Are Not Used Often

In the modern digital era, informal letters are often replaced by instant messaging and emails. Formal letters are still important in academic, official, and professional settings.

How to Write a Proper Subject Line

Be concise and specific. A subject line should clearly state the purpose of the letter. Example: "Complaint regarding damaged goods delivered on 12 July 2025."

Structure of a Formal Letter

  1. Sender's Address
  2. Date
  3. Receiver's Address
  4. Subject
  5. Salutation (e.g., Sir/Madam)
  6. Body (Introduction, Main Content, Conclusion)
  7. Closing line (e.g., Yours sincerely)
  8. Signature

Letter to the Editor – Example

Subject: Increasing Noise Pollution in Urban Areas

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing to bring attention to the growing issue of noise pollution in our city. The honking of vehicles, construction noise, and loudspeakers have made daily life extremely stressful for the residents.

This persistent noise not only affects mental peace but also poses serious health risks like hearing loss and hypertension. Students and elderly people are the worst affected as they need a peaceful environment.

Through the columns of your esteemed newspaper, I urge the authorities to implement stricter rules and raise awareness to control noise pollution. I hope this concern reaches the responsible departments soon.

Yours faithfully,
Sanskar Shep

Complaint Letter – Example

Subject: Complaint regarding delivery of defective shoes

Dear Sir/Madam,

I recently ordered a pair of sports shoes from your online portal (Order ID: 456789) which was delivered on 20th July 2025. To my disappointment, the shoes are defective with one sole completely detached.

I tried contacting your customer support, but have received no response so far. I request an immediate replacement of the product or a full refund, as per your return policy mentioned online.

Kindly resolve the issue at the earliest to maintain customer satisfaction. Looking forward to your prompt action.

Yours faithfully,
Sanskar Shep

Enquiry Letter – Example

Subject: Enquiry about summer robotics workshop for students

Dear Sir/Madam,

I came across your advertisement about a summer workshop on robotics for school students and would like to know more details regarding the program.

Please provide information about the duration, fee structure, course content, eligibility criteria, and registration deadlines. Also, let me know whether you provide any certificate upon completion.

Kindly respond at the earliest as I am interested in joining. Looking forward to your detailed reply.

Yours faithfully,
Sanskar Shep

Order Letter – Example

Subject: Order for 20 copies of Class 10 Science Textbook

Dear Sir/Madam,

We, the librarian of ABC School, wish to place an order for 20 copies of the Class 10 Science textbook (NCERT Publication) for the academic year 2025–26.

Please ensure the books are the latest edition and are delivered by 15th August 2025. The payment will be made upon delivery. Kindly confirm the availability and dispatch details soon.

We hope to receive quality service from your reputed store. Kindly consider our order urgent and deliver it on priority.

Yours faithfully,
Sanskar Shep
Librarian, ABC School
