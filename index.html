Letter to the Editor

What is it? Written to a newspaper/magazine editor to raise awareness or share opinion.

Common Lines: Through your newspaper... / I would like to draw attention...

Common Topics: Pollution, road safety, student pressure, public hygiene.

CBSE PYQ: Write a letter to editor about increasing pollution in your city (CBSE 2020)

Tips: Be factual, formal, to the point.

Example:

Format:
54, R.K. Puram
New Delhi
5 March 2025

The Editor
The Times of India
New Delhi

Subject: Increasing Pollution in Delhi

Sir/Madam,

Through the columns of your esteemed daily, I wish to express concern over the growing pollution in Delhi. Air quality is worsening, causing health issues. Strict regulations are needed.

I hope this issue is taken seriously.

Yours truly,
Sanskar Shep

Complaint Letter

What is it? Letter to report defective service/product.

Common Lines: I am writing to complain about... / I request immediate action.

Common Topics: Damaged items, electricity issues, late delivery.

CBSE PYQ: Complaint to dealer about defective fan (CBSE 2019)

Tips: Be firm but respectful, mention details.

Example:

Format:
125, Model Town
Pune
2 Feb 2025

Manager
CoolTech Appliances
Pune

Subject: Complaint Regarding Defective Ceiling Fan

Sir/Madam,

I bought a ceiling fan (Invoice no. 1422) from your store last week. It stopped working within two days. I request a replacement.

Kindly act soon.

Yours sincerely,
Sanskar Shep

Enquiry Letter

What is it? Letter to request information about course/service.

Common Lines: I wish to enquire about... / Kindly provide the following details.

Common Topics: Coaching classes, training camps, holiday packages.

CBSE PYQ: Enquire about robotics summer camp (CBSE 2021)

Tips: Mention exactly what you want to know.

Example:

Format:
2/40, Gandhi Nagar
Mumbai
10 March 2025

Director
AI Robotic Institute
Mumbai

Subject: Enquiry about Summer Robotics Camp

Sir/Madam,

I am a student of class 10 and interested in your robotics camp. Please send me information regarding duration, fee, and eligibility.

Yours faithfully,
Sanskar Shep

Job Application

What is it? Formal application for a job, attached with CV.

Common Lines: With reference to your advertisement... / I offer my candidature...

Common Topics: Teacher, typist, assistant librarian.

CBSE PYQ: Application for English teacher (CBSE 2020)

Tips: Mention qualifications, attach resume.

Example:

Format:
78, Shivaji Nagar
Nagpur
15 Jan 2025

The Principal
DAV Public School
Nagpur

Subject: Application for the post of English Teacher

Sir/Madam,

With reference to your advertisement in The Times, I wish to apply for the post of English teacher. I have completed B.A. (English) and B.Ed.

Kindly consider me.

Yours truly,
Sanskar Shep

Order Letter

What is it? Letter to place an order for goods/services.

Common Lines: Please supply the following... / Kindly send items at...

Common Topics: Books, sports goods, lab materials.

CBSE PYQ: Order 20 footballs, 10 volleyballs (CBSE 2019)

Tips: Use table format or list, mention quantity.

Example:

Format:
ABC School Beed, Maharashtra 4 July 2025

The Manager
Sports India
Pune

Subject: Order for Sports Equipment

Sir/Madam,

Kindly send the following items:

  • Footballs – 20
  • Volleyballs – 10
  • Badminton Rackets – 15
Kindly deliver within 5 days.

Yours truly,
Sanskar Shep

Request Letter

What is it? Letter asking permission/favor from authority.

Common Lines: I request you to... / Kindly permit me to...

Common Topics: TC, fee waiver, event permission, hall booking.

CBSE PYQ: Request for hall use during science fair (CBSE 2021)

Tips: Show gratitude, clearly explain reason.

Example:

Format:
85, Gandhi Chowk Aurangabad 1 Feb 2025

The Principal
Sunrise Public School
Aurangabad

Subject: Request for Hall Use

Sir/Madam,

We request permission to use the school hall for our interschool science exhibition on 10 Feb 2025. Around 150 students will participate.

Kindly approve our request.

Yours sincerely,
Sanskar Shep
