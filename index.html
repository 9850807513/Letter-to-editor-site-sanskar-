Letter Structure

To, The Editor,
The Times of India,
Mumbai.

Subject: Increasing Road Accidents Due to Negligent Driving

Sir/Madam,

Through the columns of your esteemed newspaper, I would like to express my deep concern over the increasing number of road accidents in our city due to rash and negligent driving. This issue demands immediate attention from both the authorities and citizens.

It has become difficult for pedestrians, especially children and elderly people, to walk safely. Lack of traffic sense and over-speeding are major causes. I request the concerned authorities to implement strict rules, conduct awareness drives, and take immediate steps to ensure road safety.

Thank you.

Yours sincerely,
Sanskar Bhusnar
Class 10
